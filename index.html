Current Projects

CDC MiFi® Respiratory Diagnostics (NIH SBIR)

Scalable, genome-aware detection for respiratory pathogens (e.g., measles, mumps, RSV) using e-probes and MiFi® workflows. Includes assay design, internal controls, and validation with clinical panels.

Canopy MiFi® Tick-Borne Diagnostics

Diagnostics for Cytauxzoon felis and Ehrlichia chaffeensis via HTS and e-probes. Emphasis on early detection, threshold refinement, and field validation.
