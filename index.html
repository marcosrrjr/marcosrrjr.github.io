About

Marcos Ribeiro-Junior’s interests include developing innovative tools for pathogen detection and disease diagnostics across multiple host species. By integrating advanced bioinformatics, molecular diagnostics, and high-throughput sequencing (HTS) technologies, he creates fast, accurate, and reliable diagnostic solutions for human, agricultural, and zoonotic pathogens.

Currently, he leads the development of proprietary e-probes, a core technology behind MiFi® LLC—a SaaS platform for rapid pathogen detection through HTS data analysis. His work focuses on designing, validating, and optimizing e-probes to enhance diagnostic accuracy and expand MiFi®’s detection capabilities across diverse host species.

Marcos Ribeiro-Junior received his Ph.D. in Agronomy (Plant Protection) from São Paulo State University (UNESP) in 2022, with a collaborative research period at Oklahoma State University (OSU) under the supervision of Prof. Renate Krause-Sakate and Prof. Francisco M. Ochoa-Corona. During his doctoral studies, he conducted research on molecular plant virology and bioinformatics, focusing on pathogen detection using high-throughput sequencing (HTS). Prior to that, he obtained his M.S. in Agronomy from UNESP in 2018 and his B.Sc. in Agronomy from the University of Marília (UNIMAR) in 2016.

News

I’m happy to share that I’m starting a new position as Diagnostics Specialist @ MiFi® LLC.

MiFi® is a SaaS platform for rapid pathogen detection through NGS data analysis. It utilizes pathogen-specific e-probes, designed using advanced algorithms and validation tools, to identify pathogens efficiently. Users upload NGS data, select target e-probes, and receive pathogen detection results within hours, along with a confidence interval.
